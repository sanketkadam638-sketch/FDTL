02Consecutive Days & Weekly Rest

Cabin crew must not be rostered for more than 7 consecutive duty days without a weekly rest period of at least 36 hours.

Basis & limitations: figures reflect the widely-published summary of DGCA CAR Section 7, Series J, Part I (Cabin Crew FDTL). WOCL is taken as 02:00–05:59, with a 100% reduction (capped at 2h) when a duty starts inside it and a 50% reduction when a duty ends in or spans it. The flight-time/FDP/landings table is a simplified single-axis version of the regulation; DGCA's full scheme also varies FDP by number of sectors within each band. This tool is a planning aid only — always verify against your operator's current, DGCA-approved FDTL Scheme (part of the Operations Manual), which is the sole authoritative source and may reflect a newer CAR issue.
